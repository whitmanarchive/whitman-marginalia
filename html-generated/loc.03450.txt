        Had this vol. at Wash'n 
            &amp; thro' the war— 
            —also in Camden— 
            altogether all of 20 y'rs. 
            —read it from time to time 
            write this Nov. 1889 
            in Mickle St. 
            —have been out in my 
            wheel chair for a 40 minute 
            open air jaunt (propell'd by 
            WF. my sailor boy nurse) 
            —&amp; now 4pm Nov. 14 '89 
            waiting for my supper to be 
        bro't—
