            Scythia (as used by the Greeks)
            —the northern part of Europe &amp;
            Asia
            —the people thereof "Kelts" viz
            (woods‑men
            (These were descendamtsdescendants from the same ancestors
            as the Greeks &amp; Romans themselves.)
            —another name of the above the "Umbri"
            
            —The Celtic, Teutonic &amp; Gothic races
                are all of Japhetic stock
            
            Sarmatia ancient Russia &amp;
                Poland
            
            ? "the Teutonic Races, originally from Persia"
            —"then the inhabitants of India, and the
            descendants of the Keltic and Teutonic
            nations, are all of one family, and must
            all have migrated from one country, after
                the separation of the nations of the
            —Whether that country was Persia,
            or CashmirKashmir, or a country farther east,
            is not easily determined—but it
            seems that, accordingly, the white man
            of Europe and the tawny man of India
            have a common ancestry.
